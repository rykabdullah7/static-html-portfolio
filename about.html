About Me

Hello! I’m Wajeeha Abbasi, a proud resident of Rahim Yar Khan. Currently pursuing a BS Psychology degree at NUST, I’m deeply fascinated by the human mind and behavior.

My educational journey began at The Zap School, where I completed my matriculation, followed by my intermediate education at KIPS College. Both institutions shaped my passion for learning and creativity.
